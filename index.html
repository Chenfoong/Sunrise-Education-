Our Journey

2014
Sunrise Education Founded
Established with a mission to bring happy, international-standard education to Klang, Selangor.
2016
Cambridge IGCSE Programme Launch
Began offering the full Cambridge IGCSE curriculum, bringing world-class British education to Malaysia.
2018
Homeschooling Centre Established
Launched Malaysia's warm homeschooling programme, providing flexible learning for families across Selangor.
2020
Expanded Facilities & Online Learning
Grew our campus and introduced hybrid learning options, serving more families across the region.
2023
10-Year Anniversary & New Programmes
Celebrated a decade of happy learning with new enrichment programmes and upgraded facilities.
2025
Growing Strong — New Intake Open
Continuing to inspire young hearts and empower bright futures across Klang and Selangor.
